The Biggest Greenie

Save $$, Have Fun, Make a Difference.

The Rewards

  • Save on your energy bill.
  • Learn how much energy your devices use.
  • Jump into a neighborhood conversation around getting to 100% renewable energy.

The Game

  • We help you and neighbors get started monitoring energy use.
  • On a date/time the games begin.
  • Your name is moved up or down a leaderboard based on the % energy saved.
  • Use your phone or laptop to see how you are doing and help adjust energy use.
  • After an amount of time, The Biggest Greenie is crowned.
  • All software/hardware that built this game is Open Source.

Get Started

The game is played for two months. After that, you will know the small lifestyle changes you can make as you gain a much better understanding of how the appliances in your house use or waste electricity.

About HappyDay

HappyDay is passionate about getting to 100% renewable energy through community engagement.
